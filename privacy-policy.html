Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

Privacy First: WatchBase is a completely client-side application. All your data stays on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Our Commitment to Privacy

WatchBase ("we", "our", or "us") is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your favorites, watchlist, viewing history, and preferences never leave your device. We have no servers collecting your data, no analytics tracking your behavior, and no third-party services monitoring your usage.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Unlike many apps, WatchBase does not collect or transmit:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Device identifiers or tracking information
  • Location data
  • Your favorites, watchlist, or viewing preferences
  • Any data that could identify you or your usage patterns

How Your Data is Stored

All app data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms:

  • Favorites: Stored locally on your device only
  • Watchlist: Stored locally on your device only
  • Watched History: Stored locally on your device only
  • App Settings: Stored locally on your device only
  • Recommendations: Generated locally based on your on-device data

This data is never synchronized to the cloud, never sent to our servers (we don't have any), and never shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

WatchBase uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie and TV show information, including titles, descriptions, images, and ratings. This is a read-only connection:

  • We only read public movie and TV show data from TMDB
  • We do not send any of your personal data to TMDB
  • We do not send your favorites, watchlist, or viewing history to TMDB
  • TMDB may log API requests (like any web service), but these requests contain no personal information

For more information about TMDB's practices, please refer to TMDB's Privacy Policy.

Permissions

WatchBase may request the following optional permissions:

  • Notifications: Only if you choose to enable them. Used solely to notify you about new releases or updates to shows in your watchlist. These notifications are generated locally on your device.

All permissions are optional and the app functions fully without them.

Data Security

Since all your data is stored locally on your device and never transmitted to external servers, your data security is protected by your device's built-in security features:

  • Data is protected by iOS's secure storage mechanisms
  • Data is encrypted when your device is locked (iOS standard encryption)
  • Data is only accessible by the WatchBase app on your device

Your Data, Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Deleting the app removes all associated data from your device
  • No data remains on any external servers (because we don't use any)
  • You can clear your favorites, watchlist, and history at any time within the app

Children's Privacy

WatchBase does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and no information is transmitted, the app is safe for users of all ages. However, parental guidance is recommended for content selection.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or legal requirements. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Since we don't collect your contact information, we cannot notify you directly of changes.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or WatchBase's privacy practices, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
