Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

WatchBase ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

WatchBase is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide you with the best experience:

  • Usage Data: We may collect information about how you interact with the app, such as which movies or TV shows you view, favorite, or add to your watchlist.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version to ensure compatibility and improve performance.
  • Local Storage: Your preferences, favorites, and watchlist are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide personalized recommendations based on your preferences
  • Improve the app's functionality and user experience
  • Fix bugs and ensure the app works correctly
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our services

Data Storage

Your data, including favorites, watchlist, and preferences, is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit this personal data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to sync your data (if such a feature is available).

Third-Party Services

WatchBase uses The Movie Database (TMDB) API to fetch movie and TV show information. When you use the app, requests are made to TMDB's servers. Please refer to TMDB's Privacy Policy for information about how they handle data.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of certain data collection practices

Children's Privacy

WatchBase is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
